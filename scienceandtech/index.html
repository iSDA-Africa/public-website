science & tech

Science, research & innovation.

At iSDA, our work is driven by a deep-rooted dedication to scientific excellence and enriched by a multi-disciplinary approach that fosters innovation, collaboration, and impactful solutions.

Research

Our approach.

We draw on the latest advances across multiple scientific fields, including decision science, integrated agronomic management, remote sensing, field sensors, weather services, AI, and digital technology.

Our aim is to deliver the best possible advisory solutions for smallholder farmers at the lowest possible cost.

Decision first

We focus first and foremost on identifying, analysing and supporting the critical decisions farmers and aggregators face in risky production environments, drawing on development in decision science.

Embracing uncertainty

We represent uncertainties in knowledge and data, for example using probability distributions, focusing data collection on areas that have the highest value for making better decisions, and communicating uncertainty to farmers.

Knowledge engineering

One of our greatest strengths is in the way we judiciously include and quantify all factors that are important to a decision, irrespective of data availability, and combine information from multiple sources using Bayesian Networks.

Spatial agronomy

Our cloud-based spatial agronomy platform is a fundamental resource for being able to provide a very low-cost, all-digital advisory service.

Diagnostics

We diagnose what factors are limiting yield and profitability, including deploying low-cost sensors, and seek practical solutions for removing constraints.

Measuring impact

We are committed to measuring our impacts on changes in stakeholder behaviour, yields, product quality and profits.
