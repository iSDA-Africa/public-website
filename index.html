We believe every farmer in Africa should have access to the knowledge they need to grow more, earn more and build resilient futures. That’s why we combine cutting-edge science, farmer-centred design and scalable technology to deliver agronomic advice that works.

From soil data to decision science, everything we do is designed to create lasting impact for smallholders.

Powered by artificial intelligence, Virtual Agronomist connects with farmers via WhatsApp to deliver tailored nutrient plans and agronomic advice for their fields - whenever and wherever they need it.

By combining soil nutrient data, local insights, farm history, weather forecasts and the best scientific knowledge of the crop and region, farmers can have the benefit of an agronomist visit for every field.

open data

iSDAsoil
field-level soil map for Africa.

We've built the first field-level soil map for Africa, with 20+ soil properties estimated at 30m resolution for the entire continent.

This open resource is used globally by researchers, startups, agribusiness and governments to produce advisory and analysis, and it powers Virtual Agronomist to allow advice to be given with or without a soil test.

A living resource

We've designed iSDAsoil for rapid updates and continuous improvement. Do you have African soil data that could be used to improve these maps? Get in touch!

Open for sustainability

iSDAsoil is open data - free for all and our methodology is public, it's our way of giving back to the whole ecosystem.
