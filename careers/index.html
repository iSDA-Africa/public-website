Values

We are a global team,
but work together in all we do.

We operate in a flat, high-trust, startup environment. Everyone is individually skilled, but when we come together we are more than the sum of our parts.

Benefits

Working at iSDA comes with great benefits.

Great people deserve great benefits, and so we offer a compelling package of mission, flexibility, salary and performance-related bonuses. All routinely reviewed based on team feedback.

Financial benefits

Including individual and company performance-related reward schemes, private health insurance, pension, training budget and home office stipend.

Flexible work environment

Remote-first with great flexibility in working hours and location, as well as generous holiday provision and flexibility of use.

A mission you can believe in

We believe all farmers can benefit from our technology to increase their profits and improve their livelihoods year-on-year, and that's what gets us out of bed each morning!
