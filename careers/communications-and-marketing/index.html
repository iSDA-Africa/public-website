Remote (Africa) - Digital

Refer a friend Apply

About iSDA

At iSDA we’re redefining African smallholder agriculture with Virtual Agronomist – the continent’s fastest-growing AI-powered advisory platform, delivered via WhatsApp. Launched in 2024, Virtual Agronomist has already supported over 250,000 farmers across multiple countries, boosting yields and incomes at a fraction of the cost of traditional agricultural extension. Powered by iSDAsoil – Africa’s first high-resolution soil map – the platform delivers expert, hyperlocal advice rooted in science. We’re scaling rapidly, with the goal of reaching 10 million farmers by 2030.

The Opportunity

We’re looking for a Communications & Marketing Intern to help us tell our story, expand our reach and influence, and connect with partners across Africa.

This isn’t a back-office role. You’ll be at the front line of our communications, helping shape how iSDA shows up to the world and building your skills at the same time. You’ll learn fast, take ownership and contribute to work that’s building our brand, driving engagement, and ultimately changing smallholder agriculture across the continent. If you’re ready to use – and build - your communications and marketing skills to amplify a bold mission via digital campaigns, storytelling and partnership communications, then this role is for you.

Key Responsibilities

External Communications and Storytelling

  • Ideate, write and produce content for iSDA’s website, newsletters, blog and social media – including stories, case studies, thought leadership pieces and press releases that spotlight our impact on farmers and food systems.
  • Assist with designing and running digital campaigns that grow Virtual Agronomist’s reach.
  • Develop farmer-friendly content for digital platforms, including short videos, visuals, and other engaging formats.
  • Track and analyse engagement data, using insights to sharpen campaigns and content.
  • Support development of event collateral (banners, flyers, handouts) to strengthen iSDA’s presence at conferences, workshops, and partner events.
  • Ensure our voice is consistent, confident and clear across all communications.

Internal Communications

  • Keep our remote team connected through engaging internal communications
  • Partner Communications
  • Support the development of partner-facing communications, including preparing stakeholder updates, partner materials (presentations, flyers) that inspire and inform.
  • Responding to inquiries from new or existing partners as necessary.

Farmer-facing Communications

  • Support farmer-facing communications that drive Virtual Agronomist sign-ups and deepen ongoing engagement.
  • Help strengthen how we communicate with farmers – making advice more practical, engaging, and easy to act on.

Who You Are

  • Based in Africa with reliable internet access.
  • 2–3 years of experience in communications, marketing, journalism, or a related field.
  • Strong writing and storytelling skills, with the ability to make complex topics clear and engaging.
  • Strong written and spoken English
  • Familiar with social media tools and comfortable managing digital platforms.
  • Some experience in basic design or video editing (e.g., Canva, CapCut, or similar).
  • Some experience with data analytics (social analytics, Google Analytics etc.) preferred.
  • Proactive and eager to learn, with the confidence to work independently and collaboratively in a remote team.
  • Passionate about social impact – interest in agriculture or rural development is a plus.

What We Offer

  • $1,000 monthly honorarium to support your participation during the internship.
  • A chance to develop real-world skills while making a meaningful impact on farmers’ lives.
  • Flexible, remote work environment with a diverse team across Africa.
  • Opportunity to transition into a permanent role based on performance.

How to Apply

Send your CV (no more than 2 pages) and 2 samples of your work, with one example being from the last 6 months, (e.g., article, social media post, campaign content , or design) to info@isda-africa.com with the subject line:

“Application – Communications & Marketing Intern”

Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
