Keith Shepherd (iSDA Chief Science Officer) presented a paper at the World Congress of Soil Science on A Global Soil Spectral Calibration & Estimation Service in Glasgow in August 2022.

The paper described ia freely available service that will allow users anywhere in the world to upload soil infrared spectra measurements and obtain estimates of multiple soil properties.
