iSDA exhibiting at AGRF Summit, Kigali

Grow. Nourish. Reward - Bold Actions for Resilient Food Systems

AGRF, Africa’s premier forum for driving the food and agriculture agenda in the continent, will host its 12th annual summit under the theme Grow. Nourish. Reward – Bold Actions for Resilient Food Systems from September 5-9, 2022 in Kigali, Rwanda.

We’ll be attending and exhibiting our products:

Virtual Agronomist

Directly supporting the aims of the summit is Virtual Agronomist, our system for expert advisory for farmers at scale, which supports resilient food systems by improving smallholder farmer yields and incomes, and by reducing waste through individualised fertiliser plans.

iSDAsoil

The first field-level soil map for Africa, iSDAsoil provides soil property estimates for the entire continent at 30m resolution. It’s an open resouce used globally by researchers, startups, agribusiness and governments to produce advisory, analysis and policy.

Resilient food systems begin by understanding the soil beneath each farm, and we’re proud to be part of the solution for Africa.
