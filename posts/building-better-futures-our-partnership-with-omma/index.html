Building Better Futures: Our Partnership With OMMA Ltd.

Published on November 20, 2024
Share:

At iSDA, we pride ourselves on using cutting-edge agricultural technologies to empower farmers and improve livelihoods. It is with great enthusiasm that we announce our partnership with OMMA Ltd, a unique ecosystem that enhances food security, promotes sustainability, and stimulates economic growth, based in Kigali, Rwanda.

This collaboration marks a significant step forward in delivering precision agriculture tools directly to those who need them most: smallholder maize and rice farmers.

For these farmers, we will be launching Virtual Agronomist, an AI-powered advisory service designed to enhance productivity, sustainability, and profitability. In 2025, this service will reach 1,000 farmers, expanding to 2,000 by 2026. Through actionable insights on fertiliser optimisation, agronomic practices, and nutrient management, we aim to unlock untapped potential in their fields.

For OMMA Ltd, this partnership promises improved yield quality and volume, precise forecasting, and enhanced operational efficiency. For farmers, the benefits are life-changing: increased profitability, better resource utilisation, and the hope for a more secure future.

This collaboration isn’t just about technology; it’s about community. Field officers and lead farmers will act as local champions, bridging the gap between innovation and implementation.

As we embark on this journey with OMMA Ltd, we remain committed to our mission: delivering solutions that make agriculture smarter, more sustainable, and inclusive. Together, we’re sowing the seeds for a brighter future.
