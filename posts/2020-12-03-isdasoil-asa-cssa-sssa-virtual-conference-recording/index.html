iSDAsoil at the ASA-CSSA-SSSA Virtual Conference

Published on December 3, 2020
Share:

To celebrate the launch of iSDAsoil, the iSDA team made two presentations at The American Society of Agronomy, the Crop Science Society of America, and the Soil Science Society of America (ASA-CSSA-SSSA) Conference. This international virtual conference ran from the 9-13 November 2020.

Achim Dobermann of the iSDA Board presented an overview of iSDAsoil. He outlined the problem space, importance of the breakthrough, and its potential applications:

Achim Dobermann presents iSDAsoil

The iSDA team then held an in-depth interactive session on iSDAsoil. This consisted of a live demo of iSDAsoil’s functionality, an expert panel discussion, and Q&A. The panel discussion members were:

Ranveer Chandra: Chief Scientist, Microsoft Azure Global
Christy Van Beek: Director, SoilCares Foundation and Chief Agronomist, Agrocares
Nathan Price: Crop and Agronomy Director, Yara
