Our Impact

Driving change. Sustainably.

At iSDA, we work with smallholder farmers, local groups, and governments across Africa to help farmers produce healthier crops, unlock new opportunities and reach broader markets.

COMMUNITY

Farmers first.

iSDA creates easy-to-use, data-powered tools with an aim to sustainably boost smallholder farmer incomes and make smart agriculture accessible and affordable for farmers across the Global South.

With products designed around local needs, we empower farmers with practical insights and technology to drive productivity, resilience, and lasting growth.

PRINCIPLES

What it takes.

iSDA was founded by three research institutes: Rothamsted Research, the World Agroforestry Centre (ICRAF) and the International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (IITA).

We are a mission-driven company that carries forward the legacy of the African Soils Information Service (AfSIS).

SERVICE

Accessible design.

Our Virtual Agronomist AI agronomic advisor makes farming advice easy and accessible. Farmers can simply send a WhatsApp message: no apps, no hassle. It's as simple as chatting with a real agronomist.

Farmers can ask questions, get tailored advice, and manage their crops, all in a way that's easy, affordable, and right at their fingertips. No costly land surveyors or fancy tech needed!
