Our Impact

Driving change. Sustainably.

At iSDA, we work with smallholder farmers, local groups, and governments across Africa to help farmers produce healthier crops, unlock new opportunities and reach broader markets.

COMMUNITY

Farmers first.

iSDA creates easy-to-use, data-powered tools with an aim to sustainably boost smallholder farmer incomes and make smart agriculture accessible and affordable for farmers across the Global South.

With products designed around local needs, we empower farmers with practical insights and technology to drive productivity, resilience, and lasting growth.

SERVICE

Accessible design.

Our Virtual Agronomist AI advisor makes expert farming guidance easy and accessible. Farmers just send a WhatsApp message - no apps, no fuss. It’s as simple as chatting with a real agronomist, with valuable information right at their fingertips.

With affordable, on-the-spot advice, there’s no need for expensive tools or land surveys. Plus, lead farmers can register plots to extend our reach, helping connect more farmers to valuable support.

OUTLOOK

Sustainable future.

Virtual Agronomist simplifies sustainable farming by providing farmers with clear, personalised advice. Leveraging AI, iSDAsoil, and our scientific knowledge base, it offers tailored tips to manage nutrients, control pests, and boost crop health.

With seasonal reminders and real-time guidance, farmers can enhance productivity, conserving resources for a sustainable future. Virtual Agronomist also supports cost sustainability by providing a digital extension service that is 10% more affordable than traditional extension.
