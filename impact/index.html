iSDA exists to transform smallholder farming – using science, data and partnerships to deliver high-quality advice at massive scale and low cost. At the centre of this is Virtual Agronomist, our AI-powered platform that delivers customised, plot-level guidance to farmers via WhatsApp – already used by over 250,000 farmers across seven African countries. Here, we set out how we’ll achieve impact at scale – reaching millions of farmers, transforming national extension systems and unlocking a future of sustainable farming for all.

What We Stand For

Beyond Harvests. Our work doesn’t stop at yield increases – we focus on long-term resilience, farmer income, soil health and system-wide transformation.

Our Impact in Year One

Behind this growth is a simple idea: make expert agronomic advice radically accessible - via a phone farmers already own, in the language they actually speak.

Our Vision

10 Million Farmers. One Powerful Platform.

By 2030, we aim to reach over 10 million African farmers with AI-supported, scientifically sound, farm-specific advice. But this is about more than scale - it’s about systems transformation.

This means:

  • Moving from one-size-fits-all extension to farm-level precision.
  • Replacing one-off field visits with real-time, season-long advice.
  • Shifting advisory from expensive and reactive, to open, adaptive and radically low-cost.
  • We’re proving a new model for agricultural extension that’s 10× more scalable, 10× cheaper, and actually used by the farmers it’s built for. It’s digital infrastructure for African food systems - and it works.

Built by Scientists. Proven on the Farm.

AI Meets Agronomy: Advice You Can Trust

Virtual Agronomist is underpinned by the most detailed soil and crop models in Africa, combining:

  • iSDASoil’s high-resolution maps
  • Nutrient recommendation engines tailored to specific plots
  • AI-driven diagnosis of pests, planting windows, and practices
  • Real-world validation via trials and farmer data

It’s science-led, not tech-for-tech’s-sake. Every recommendation is backed by data and evidence — and continuously improves with every farmer interaction.

Smart, Inclusive Scaling: From Maize to Coffee

Not All Farmers Grow the Same - So We Don’t Treat Them the Same.

Virtual Agronomist is designed around two core farmer realities:

  • Staple crop farmers need support to break cycles of low fertility and food insecurity.
  • Cash crop farmers need precision tools to maximise profit and quality.
  • Virtual Agronomist tailors its offering:
    • Free, resilience-first advice for staple crops like maize and sorghum
    • Premium or bundled features for commercial crops like tea, coffee and cocoa
    • Models that cross-subsidise advisory, ensuring access doesn’t depend on income

Open by Design

Not Just a Tool - A Platform for Innovation

At iSDA we’re not just building tools, we’re building platforms for others to build on. Our “T-shaped” strategy means going deep where we lead (agronomic AI), and wide where others innovate:

  • Open APIs for nutrient recommendations, pest diagnosis, chatbot logic
  • Public access to iSDAsoil maps and crop image libraries
  • Developer-friendly documentation and sandbox environments
  • Data-sharing partnerships

This is agricultural infrastructure - not intellectual property. We want governments, NGOs, and startups to build with us, not compete with us.

Farming for 2030, Not 1990
