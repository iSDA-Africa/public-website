Putting knowledge
in your hands

Virtual Agronomist uses artificial intelligence to communicate directly with farmers through WhatsApp, providing tailored nutrient plans and agronomic advice for their plots.

Based on soil nutrient status, local knowledge of yields, farm history, forecasts, economics and the best scientific knowledge of the crop and region, farmers can have the benefit of an agronomist visit for every field.
